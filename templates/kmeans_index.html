understanding k-means:

K-Means is an unsupervised learning algorithm that groups similar data points together. It works by iteratively updating cluster centers (centroids) until they stabilize. The algorithm minimizes the within-cluster sum of squares, making points within each cluster as similar as possible. Use the slider to see how centroids move and clusters form over iterations!
